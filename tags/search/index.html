Search with hugo

I’ve added a /search function to the blog. I’ve been more often here and did some search of posts of my own. Because I do not like to have google or something else to search for my content I wished to add a search function. The hugo documentation has a page for this matters. It lists various solutions to implement. I took the one without NPM or elastic search. It was implemented by a guy called ‘eddiewebb’ and does come with a simple: ‘Place this files here’ solution Basically, during the creation process of the pages with hugo, it will index the content.
