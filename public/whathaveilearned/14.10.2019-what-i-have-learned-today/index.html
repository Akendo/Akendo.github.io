What I have learned

Today, I have learned that emotion can not be detected reliable.

The classical view of since about emotion are, that emotion are clearly distinguishable. Just like a fingerprint we could sort a given emotion to a fitting expression. However, it’s not possible to reliable detect emotion based on faces expression.

Instead emotion are learned though the ages. This is the theory of constructed emotions.
