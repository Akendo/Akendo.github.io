Writing ahead

A Happy new year everyone, this is a late one! I have a bit of an announcement to make: There will be more blog posts! This Blog should be used more often and I’m going to develop a better habit of writing thoughts and current topic in here.

The blog was created initially as a side project, but sufferers from a leak of attention. Most posts do not have the quality to be published in my view and because everything should be more as perfect, but I do not publish posts at all. But the writing helps to gather thoughts and ideas about a topic and aids in becoming more focused about and this is what matters more.

Until this point writing has been a very vulnerable process of mine. Sharing composed texts with others feel like sharing a part of me. A friend of mine phrased it as the following: The fear of publishing.

Even now it takes way more time than planed, it should have been a short post and I’m spending more than half an hour on this text…but writing is hard, isn’t? However, many ideas and thought I had about a posts could have been an immense help in the past, because it adds the power to talk about.

What’s next?

So what should be written in here?

In one way I’m going share some of my internal documentation composed over the last years. I quite often document things, but not share them. It’s mostly one or two lines of written text, however, it contributes over time a lot of value. On the other side, I’m going to write some more opinions and views I have.

How will this look like? There should a post about something each week. At the moment this is intended for Monday, however, this might change.

So far akendo
