People were appalled about this. “What using a piece of paper to do what?". The manfacture of the product creates specially advertisment that try to teach people the benefit of these products.

I think that they succeded.

How our thinking affects our behaviour.

In one instance how we choose convient over effectiveness. Because it seems more effect on the short term than on the long term.

The othere instance is that effectiveness of advertisment.

An example with advertisment

advertisment is just similar to the paper towels, not all of it, but most of it.

The worst part of advertisment is, that it tries to create a need where none is.

advertisment causes us to consume more than we really need.

The most important one was: None want so evaluate the advertisment seen on our screen. While we have the technogly to do so, the industir is very well aware of it’s own ineffectness. However, it’s a buisness that is running, so why change the rules?
